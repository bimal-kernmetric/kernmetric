Systems vs Symptoms

Most brands optimize symptoms.
We diagnose systems.

Ads, conversion funnels, email sequences, pricing strategies, and landing pages are downstream outputs. The real constraint sitting higher inside the system goes completely unaddressed.

01 / ACQUISITION FALLACY

Scaling Meta Ads

Scaling ad accounts seems like the growth driver, but high customer acquisition costs (CAC) typically subsidize flat or negative contribution margins hidden in product cohorts.

02 / RETENTION FRICTION

Email & Churn Portals

Retention portals and discount sequences treat subscription cancellations as marketing anomalies, missing physical integration hurdles and routine friction constraints.

03 / FINANCIAL COGNITION

Omnichannel Margin Subsidy

High-velocity DTC stores aggressively fund inventory cash cycles, masking slotting fees, distribution penalties, and flat unit profitability in physical retail stores.
